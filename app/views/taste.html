Taste of Costa Rica - Itenerary

B: breakfast, L: lunch, D: dinner.

1st day: After you exit the terminal at the international airport in San José you will be greeted by our representative who will then transfer you to the Arenal Volcano.. On the way you will be seen a lot of different plantations. Naturalist Guide and Driver will be with you all the time. B and L included.

2nd day: Arenal. Some activities to do in Arenal: Canopy tour . Suspension bridges. Canyoning tour Horseback riding. Hot spring water . Bird watching. Caño Negro Wildlife Refuge. Ecological farm Don Juan. Chocolate tour. B and L included.

3rd day: After breakfast you`ll start your transfer up to Monteverde Cloud Forest. After lunch you will be hiking into the cloud forest as longer you like. B and L included.

4thday: More activities to do in Monteverde: ATV tours. Horse Back Riding. Twilight tour. Butterfly farm. Ecological Sanctuary. Frog Pound. B and L included.

5th day:After breakfast, mid-morning departure by car and also crossing the Golf of Nicoya via ferry to Playa Tambor for two nights. Beautiful Hotel with private beach, lookout point to enjoy the sunset. B and L included.

6th day: More activities at the area: Isla Tortuga. Kayak. Snorkeling. Curu Wildlife Refuge. Horseback riding. B included.

7th day: At leisure.

8th day: This morning, at convenient time, you’ll have a private transfer to the International Airport, where you will board your flight back home. Have a nice trip back home. Get in contact with us for more details.
