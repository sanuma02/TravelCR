Danaus Eco Center

Danaus is without a doubt a nature paradise, like a nature oasis surrounded by pasture, rice and pineapple plantations.

Within the small ecological reserve which regenerated naturally, thanks to the efforts of a group of people concerned about conservation of our environment and education of our children and adults of the outlying areas, is how this wonderful project was bornÂ  and cooperated with the tourist development of the town of La Fortuna. Also they included training and sustainable production programs, tree nursery of native species in danger of extinction.

In it we will find a Botanical Garden whit a lot of species, Butterfly gardens were you can see all their circle of life: eggs, larvae and chrysalides. Whit their own spring water forming a small lagoon you can see birds and reptiles. There are areas destined to frog reproduction in a natural way, and areas dedicated to the exhibition of medicinal plants.

Wild life like butterflies, Three and Two Toed Sloths, Agouties, Caiman, Basilisks, Blue Jeans Poison Dart Frog and around 100 species of birds are commonly found at the area.
