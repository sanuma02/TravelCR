Welcome to travel with friends Costa Rica

We offer different options to get to know our country! Traveling with friends and experience the beauty Costa Rica offers

Traditional - Itenerary

Breakfast (B), Lunch (L), Dinner (D).

1st day: After you exit the terminal at the international airport you will be greeted by our representative who will then transfer you to your hotel, only 20 minutes from the airport.

2nd day: Today your guide will meet you at the hotel lobby to start your adventure. First you are going to visit the active Poas Volcano, on the way you will have the opportunity to stop at the coffee plantations and learn about the process. Once you enjoy the beauty of the area you will start driving to Arenal Volcano., traveling true mountains and valleys, for around 2 and a half hours, stopping for lunch served in a family house, were you also can learn how to prepare “tortillas”. B and L included.

3rd day: You are welcome to enjoy the hotel gardens before breakfast, searching for birds, lizards, monkeys and more. After breakfast gets ready for your first hike in the rain forest, Suspension Bridges were you have chances to see a lot of wildlife like birds, monkeys, reptiles and great views of the volcano. At the afternoon you are going to enjoy Canyoning tour. After a long day full of activities you deserve a relaxing time: Hot spring water, is a wonderful and authentic environment activity that offers both privacy and tranquility. B and L included.

4thday: After breakfast you'll be transported to the Arenal lake were you are going to have a very smooth boat trip for 40 minutes, at the end a car will be transfer you to Monteverde Cloud Forest, arriving on time for lunch at local restaurant. This afternoon time to fly, feel like Tarzan and Superman, one of the most famous attractions at the area: Canopy Tour, full of adrenaline. B and L included.

5th day:Before breakfast you are welcome to enjoy with your guide the hotel gardens, looking for birds and mammals. After breakfast you will be driving for 10 minutes to the cloud forest were you are going to walk for around 3 hours discovering the greatness of the area. Afternoon at leisure to enjoy the hotel facilities. The area offers you a lot of attraction, ask your guide for options. B and L included.

6th day: These morning you can enjoy the hotel facilities and live at any time to Manuel Antonio National Park, located in the middle pacific coast. During your transfer you will enjoy a scenic drive and your guide will be on the lookout for wildlife such as iguanas, riverside crocodiles, shorebirds, scarlet macaws and more. B and L included.

7th day: Today after breakfast you will be driving only 5 minutes to the park entrance. Time to enjoy a wonderful walk for around 2 hours and then you can stay at the beaches as longer you want. Afternoon at leisure. B included.

8th day: Your trip has come to an end. At convenient time your guide will be transferring you to the international airport. The airlines companies require been 3 hours ahead of your departure time. B included.

Have a nice trip back home
