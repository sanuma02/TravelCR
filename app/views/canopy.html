Canopy Tour

Time to fly, you are going to enjoy the famous canopy tour. Is a system of cables of different heights and extension, were you will be flying from platform to platform true the forest, where you are going to wear pulleys, globes and helmet.

This tour is suitable for anyone over the age of 8, but for safety reason, a weight restriction of 280 pounds, a maximum waste width of 120 cm, and maximum thigh width of 80 cm must apply. This tour lasts approximately 2.5 hours. Does not require any kind of particular athletic ability. You only need to be able to sit down, remain calm, and focus your attention on the zip line you’re about to travel down—guides and gravity do the rest.

Is an adventure tour full of adrenaline above the treetops that allows you to observe the rainforest and its landscape from a different perspective and safely.

Before you go flying down the zip lines, your guides, who are well training, will teach you the proper techniques, including how to hold onto the cable and how to position your body. They will make sure that your high quality gear is properly adjusted and help you launch off from each platform. Soon after, the flight through the forest begins. Have fun.
