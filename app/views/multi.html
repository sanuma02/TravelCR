Multisport - Itenerary

B: Breakfast, L: Lunch, D: Dinner. Day 1: After you exit the terminal at the international airport you will be greeted by our representative who will then transfer you to your hotel, only 20 minutes from the airport. Hotel El Rodeo or similar. 1 night. B and L included.

Day 2: Early morning be ready to start your first great adventure: Pacuare white water rafting, a tour full of adrenaline and scenic views from the very beginning. Once you finish you will transfer to Sarapiquí area, Hotel Selva Verde or similar. B and L included. 1 night.

Day 3: You have the great opportunity to practice mountain bike true of the beaten track trails, jump into the rivers and have a tour full of adrenaline, also opportunity to see wildlife. You will be biking for around 4 hours around Sarapiquí town. Today afternoon we have chances to walk around the hotel trails or just relax. B and L included.

Day 4: On the way to Arenal Volcano, we are going to have a great tour: Chocolate tour. Time to learn a lot about Cacao seeds, drink of the gods. At the afternoon we are going to hike at the Suspension Bridges. Hotel Observatory Lodge or similar. 2 nights. B and L included.

Day 5: Ready for a great hike up to sleeping volcano: Cerro Chato. This is a hard hike, up all the time, uneven steps, holding tree roots to pull up. Once you rich the top will be a spectacular view, you`ll be descending to the crater, were you can swim in the lake, surrounding by a lush cloud forest. Your hike continues true different path, you will find a great waterfall, nice please to relax. After a day full of activities is time to enjoy the Hot spring water . B, L and D included.

Day 6:Before we start our journey to Rincon de la Vieja Volcano is time to get wet again: Canyoning tour. Hotel Hacienda Guachipelin or similar. 2 nights. B, L and D included.

Day 7: Hike at the park for around 6 hours, visiting waterfalls and areas with volcanic activities. At the afternoon we are going to relax at the hot spring water or the pool. B, L and D included.

Day 8: After breakfast you will be transfer to the beach. Afternoon at leisure. Hotel Bosque del Mar at Playa Hermosa, Guanacaste or similar. 2 nights. B included.

Day 9: At leisure. B included.

Day 10: Back home. At convenient time you will be transfer to the airport in Liberia, Guancaste. B included. Have a nice trip back home. Contact us for more information.
