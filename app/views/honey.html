Welcome to travel with friends Costa Rica

We offer different options to get to know our country! Traveling with friends and experience the beauty Costa Rica offers

HoneyMooners Option 1 - Itenerary

B: breakfast, L: lunch, D: dinner.

1st day: After you exit the terminal at the international airport in San José you will be greeted by our representative who will then transfer you to your hotel, only 20 minutes from the airport.

2nd day: Early morning be ready to start your first great adventure: Pacuare white water rafting, a tour full of adrenaline and scenic views from the very beginning. This river is the best in the country for this activity, with professional guides your tour will be one in a class. Overnight at Pacuare Lodge. B, L and D included.

3rd day: After great breakfast your second day of white water adventures starts. Today be ready for class III and IV rapids, a lot of adrenaline. At the end of your tour your professional driver guide will be transferring you to your next destination: Arenal Volcano.. Hotel Arenal Nayara or similar. 2 nights. B and L included.

4thday: After breakfast be ready for a different activity: hiking into the rain forest for around 3 hours: Suspension Bridges. Great experience. For the afternoon you are going to feel like Tarzan: Canopy tour , a great adventure true the top of the trees. After a day full of activities time to relax at the Hot spring water . B and L included.

5th day: These morning you decide if you like to stay at the hotel or ask your guide for other activities at the area. After lunch you will be transfer to a small and charming hotel up to the cloud forest: Hotel Villa Blanca. 1 night. B and L included

6th day: These morning you can enjoy the hotel facilities and live at any time to Manuel Antonio National Park , located in the middle pacific coast. During your transfer you will enjoy a scenic drive and your guide will be on the lookout for wildlife such as iguanas, riverside crocodiles, shorebirds, scarlet macaws and more. Hotel Arenas del Mar or similar. 2 nights. B and L included.

7th day: After breakfast you will be driving only 5 minutes to the park entrance. Time to enjoy a wonderful walk for around 2 hours and then you can stay at the beaches as longer you decide. Afternoon at leisure. B and L included.

8th and 9th days:You will enjoy a great Catamaran tour, searching for dolphins, turtles and whales. Dinner will be served while you enjoy the sunset. Afternoon at leisure. B included

10th day: Back home. At convenient time you will be picking up and transfer to the airport. The airlines companies require been 3 hours ahead of your departure time. B included. Have a nice trip back home.

HoneyMooners Option 2 - Itenerary

B: breakfast, L: lunch, D: dinner.

1st day: After you exit the terminal at the international airport in San José you will be greeted by our representative who will then transfer you to your hotel, only 20 minutes from the airport.

2nd day: Early morning be ready to start your first great adventure: Pacuare white water rafting, a tour full of adrenaline and scenic views from the very beginning. This river is the best in the country for this activity, with professional guides your tour will be one in a class. Overnight at Pacuare Lodge. B, L and D included.

3rd day: You are welcome to enjoy the hotel gardens before breakfast, searching for birds, lizards and monkeys and more. After great breakfast your second day of white water adventures starts. Today be ready for class III and IV rapids, a lot of adrenaline, just fallow the guide instructions and have fun. Once you finish your driver guide will be driving you to your next charming hotel surrounding by sugar cane and forest. One night. Overnight at Hotel Casa Turire. B, L and D included.

4thday: After delicious breakfast you will transported to the pier, were you are going to board a boat and transported to Tortuguero National Park. This is a place full of flora and fauna. Be ready to be in direct contact with nature, traveling by boat through the canals and also learn a lot about turtles visiting the Sea Turtles Conservancy: www.conserveturtles.org. 2 nights. Hotel Manatus. B, L and D included.

5th day: We have a lot of activities for you today, you can choose between all the options that your guide will inform you about. B, L and D included.

6th day: After breakfast take a small airplane to San Josè. From here your Guide will be driving you down to the Pacific lowlands. During your transfer you will enjoy a scenic drive and your guide will be on the lookout for wildlife such as iguanas, riverside crocodiles, shorebirds, scarlet macaws and more. Hotel Arenas del Mar. Your hotel is located very close to Manuel Antonio National Park . B and L included.

7th day: After breakfast you will be driving only 5 minutes to the park entrance. Time to enjoy a wonderful walk for around 2 hours and then you can stay at the beaches as longer you want. For today afternoon and the next 2 days ask your guide about options of tour at the area, or of course you can just relax at the beach and/or at the hotel pool and facilities. B and L included.

8th and 9th days:At leisure. B included.

10th day: At convenient time you will be picking up and transfer to the international airport. The airlines companies require to be at the airport 3 hours ahead of your departure time. B included. Have a nice trip back home.
