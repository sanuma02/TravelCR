One Day Tours - Itenerary

If you decide to spend time around San Jose area, we have some tours suggestions:

City tour: Half day tour walking and/or driving to the downtown of San José. You have the opportunity to visit the beautiful National Museum and National Theater, 2 great buildings with a lot of history. Also the central market were you can see a lot of small shops with all kind of fruit, vegetables, flowers, food, spices. Walk true the central Avenue with a great and safe boulevard. Lunch included.

Poas volcano, Grecia and Sarchi: After 2 hours driving you will be arriving to the impressive Poas volcano national park. On the way you have the opportunity to visit a coffee plantation, were you can learn a lot about this crop and enjoy the great views. Once you finish your visit to the volcano, you will depart to the little town of Grecia, on the way you will stop for lunch. In Grecia you will visit the only metallic church en Latin America. The church has a deep red color. La Catedral de las Mercedes is a gothic style church, built with imported metal from Belgium in 1890. Is so beautiful inside and imposing outside, and surrounded by the town well known like the cleanest one in Latin America. After Grecia you are going to have the opportunity to visit an Oxcart Factory, unique in the all country. Learn how the oxcarts are built, and the importance and relationship between oxcart, coffee and the culture. Lunch included.

Irazu, Lankaster gardens and Orosi Valley:After 2.5 hours driving from San José, you will be up to 3.400 mts. (11000 ft), at the summit of Irazú Volcano. On the way you will see a lot of plantations like carrots, beets, onion, garlic, dairy farms and much more. Once you are at the summit you can walk around exploring the beauty of the volcano. Living early in the morning you have more chances to have better views, maybe you can see Caribbean Sea and Pacific Ocean. After you spend some time at the Irazu Volcano, you will be driving dawn to Cartago city, the old capital, and the cathedral home of the Virgin of Los Angeles the patron saint of Costa Rica. From there you will driving to the small valley of Orosi, whit a beautifull lake and a lot of flavor. Also a short visit to Lankester Gardens were you can admire the beauty of one of the largest orchids collections. Lunch included.

White water rafting: Sarapiquì river class II and III half day: After 2 hours from San Josè driving true the spectacular Braulio Carrillo National Park rainforest you`ll be arriving to the put in to start your great adventure, white water rafting. Ready for action and get wet, is a unique experience. Around 2 pm you will be back to your hotel. Lunch included.

White water rafting: class III and IV, Pacuare River, class III and IV. : If you want to have a real action will be in this tour. In the Pacuare River takes place the white water world championship, and also well known like the river with the most beautiful scenery. These are all day tour, living around 6:30 am and returning around 5 pm. Breakfast and lunch will be provided.

Carara National Park: Carara National Park is located in the middle pacific only one and a half hours driving, is a transition forest between dry and rainforest. Is located only up to 120 ft. Near to the big Tárcoles River, home of a lot of crocodiles, egrets, herons, flycatchers, spoonbills and more. A lot of species of birds like macaws, toucans, wrens, warblers, woodpecker, wood creeper and a lot of mammals are founded in Carara.

Organization for Tropical Studies (OTS): One hour away from San Jose, located in the lowlands of the Caribbean slopes, 150 ft up, is a place were hundreds of biologists had been doing their research over there. Originally the place was belong to the famous biologist Daniel Janzen, and now is run by the O.T.S. Walking around the trails in wonderful conditions you may see a lot of birds and mammals, you can have the opportunity to meet people like students, professors, scientist from different parts of the world and you can inter act with them.
